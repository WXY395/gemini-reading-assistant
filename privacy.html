Gemini Reading Assistant

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-18 · Last updated: 2026-04-18 · Version 3.0.3
← Home Support Terms GitHub

1. Overview

Gemini Reading Assistant (the "Extension") is a Chrome extension that adds a reading layer — navigation, search, citation, export, and screenshot tools — to conversations on gemini.google.com. This policy explains exactly what data the Extension touches, where it is stored, and what (if anything) leaves your device.

Short version: Everything stays on your computer except a single license-verification call to Gumroad when you activate a Pro license.

2. What data the Extension handles

Data Where it is stored Purpose
Extension settings (which modules are enabled, Gemini plan selector, custom prompts) chrome.storage.local on your device Remember your preferences between sessions.
Citation cards and memory pins (text you explicitly save) chrome.storage.local on your device Let you reuse saved passages inside Gemini.
Conversation snapshots & journals (auto-save of visible messages) chrome.storage.local on your device Restore context and enable Markdown / TXT / JSON export.
Pro license data (license code, verification timestamp, purchase email returned by Gumroad) chrome.storage.local on your device Unlock Pro features locally without repeated verification.
Screenshot images (Ctrl+Shift+X feature) Never stored. Downloaded directly to your device via the browser's native download mechanism. Let you save parts of a conversation as PNG files.

3. Data transmitted outside your device

The Extension makes exactly one type of outbound network call:

  • Gumroad license verification — When you activate or silently re-verify a Pro license, the Extension sends the license key and a hardcoded product ID to https://api.gumroad.com/v2/licenses/verify. Gumroad returns whether the license is valid and (optionally) the purchase email so the UI can show the account owner. No conversation content is ever transmitted.

The Extension does not use any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, advertising SDK, or external logging service.

4. Permissions and why they are requested

Permission Why it is needed
storage Save settings, citation cards, memory pins, conversation journals, and license state locally in chrome.storage.local.
activeTab (added in v3.0.3) Power the screenshot feature (chrome.tabs.captureVisibleTab). Invoked only when you click a popup button or press Ctrl+Shift+X. Captured images are processed entirely in the browser and downloaded as PNG — never uploaded.
https://gemini.google.com/* (host permission) Inject the reading layer on Gemini pages. The Extension reads visible DOM text only; it does not intercept Gemini's internal APIs.
https://api.gumroad.com/* (host permission) Send the Pro license verification request described in Section 3.

5. Cookies, tracking, and advertising

The Extension does not set cookies, does not track you across websites, does not fingerprint your device, and does not display advertising.

6. Children's privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your rights and controls

  • View or export your data. Use the popup's Export JSON button to download every piece of stored data as a single JSON file.
  • Delete your data. Uninstalling the Extension removes everything stored under chrome.storage.local. You can also clear individual pieces from the popup (citation cards, memory pins) or deactivate your Pro license via the popup.
  • Deactivate Pro license. Click Deactivate license in the popup to remove the local license record.

8. Data retention

All data is stored locally and persists only as long as you keep the Extension installed. Uninstalling the Extension triggers Chrome's standard extension-data cleanup. The Extension also auto-purges temporary handoff data after 60 seconds as a safety measure.

9. Third parties

  • Google Gemini — The Extension operates on gemini.google.com, which has its own privacy policy governing how Google handles your conversations.
  • Gumroad — Pro purchases and license verification go through Gumroad, which has its own privacy policy at gumroad.com/privacy.

10. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected by bumping the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, when significant, noted in the release notes of the corresponding Extension version.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or data-deletion requests: open an issue at github.com/W8964/gemini-reading-assistant/issues.

1. 概述

Gemini Reading Assistant（下稱「本擴充功能」）是在 gemini.google.com 對話頁面上加入導航、搜尋、引用、匯出、 截圖等閱讀輔助功能的 Chrome 擴充。本政策說明本擴充接觸到哪些資料、 儲存在哪裡、以及（若有）哪些資料會離開你的裝置。

簡短版：所有資料都在你本機瀏覽器內，唯一的對外連線 是當你啟用 Pro 授權時向 Gumroad 發送的授權驗證請求。

2. 本擴充處理的資料

資料儲存位置用途
擴充功能設定（模組開關、Gemini plan 選擇、自訂 prompt） 本機 chrome.storage.local 記住你的偏好設定。
引用卡與記憶錨點（你主動儲存的文字） 本機 chrome.storage.local 在 Gemini 中再次使用已保存的段落。
對話快照與 journal（可見訊息的自動保存） 本機 chrome.storage.local 還原上下文、支援 Markdown / TXT / JSON 匯出。
Pro 授權資料（授權碼、驗證時間戳、Gumroad 回傳的購買 email） 本機 chrome.storage.local 在本機解鎖 Pro 功能，避免每次都要連線驗證。
截圖圖片（Ctrl+Shift+X 功能） 完全不儲存，直接透過瀏覽器原生下載機制下載到你的裝置。 讓你把對話片段存成 PNG 檔。

3. 離開裝置的資料

本擴充只會發出一種對外網路請求：

  • Gumroad 授權驗證：當你啟用或背景重新驗證 Pro 授權時， 本擴充會將授權碼與寫死的產品 ID 傳送至 https://api.gumroad.com/v2/licenses/verify。Gumroad 會回傳 授權是否有效、以及（可選）購買 email 讓 UI 顯示帳戶持有者。 對話內容永遠不會被傳送。

本擴充不使用任何分析工具、遙測、崩潰回報、廣告 SDK 或外部記錄服務。

4. 權限與理由

權限用途
storage 在本機 chrome.storage.local 儲存設定、引用卡、記憶錨點、對話 journal、授權狀態。
activeTab （v3.0.3 新增） 支援截圖功能（chrome.tabs.captureVisibleTab）。僅在你點擊 popup 按鈕或按 Ctrl+Shift+X 時才會呼叫。截圖圖片完全在瀏覽器內處理後下載為 PNG，絕不上傳。
https://gemini.google.com/* 在 Gemini 頁面注入閱讀層。本擴充僅讀取可見 DOM 文字，不攔截 Gemini 的內部 API。
https://api.gumroad.com/* 發送第 3 節所述的 Pro 授權驗證請求。

5. Cookie、追蹤與廣告

本擴充不設定 Cookie、不進行跨站追蹤、不做裝置指紋識別、不顯示任何廣告。

6. 兒童隱私

本擴充不以 13 歲以下兒童為對象，亦不會刻意蒐集兒童資料。

7. 你的權利與控制

  • 檢視或匯出資料：點擊 popup 的「匯出 JSON」將所有儲存資料下載為單一 JSON 檔。
  • 刪除資料：解除安裝本擴充會一併刪除所有儲存在 chrome.storage.local 的資料。你也可以在 popup 清除個別引用卡、記憶錨點，或取消 Pro 授權。
  • 取消 Pro 授權：在 popup 點擊「取消授權」即可移除本機授權記錄。

8. 資料保留

所有資料皆為本機儲存，僅在你仍安裝本擴充功能時存在。解除安裝會觸發 Chrome 標準的 擴充資料清除流程。此外，臨時的 handoff 資料會在 60 秒後自動清除作為安全措施。

9. 第三方

  • Google Gemini：本擴充運作於 gemini.google.com，Google 對你的對話有自己的隱私政策。
  • Gumroad：Pro 購買與授權驗證透過 Gumroad 進行，其隱私政策見 gumroad.com/privacy。

10. 政策變更

任何實質變更會反映在本頁頂部的「Last updated」日期；若變更重大，將同步寫入對應擴充版本的 release notes。

11. 聯絡方式

隱私相關問題或資料刪除請求，請至 github.com/W8964/gemini-reading-assistant/issues 開 issue。
